Guidelines on the Submission of Enrollment Documents

1.Pack the original copy of the following documents in a long brown envelope: • SF9 / F-138 (Learners Progress Report Card) • PSA-issued Birth Certificate • Certificate of Good Moral Character (CGMC)

• Certificate of INC Membership/Patotoo ng Local (CINCM) [For INC Members Only] • Passport [For Foreign Nationality or Students From Foreign Schools Only] • Special Study Permit from the Bureau of Immigration [For Foreign Nationality or Students from Foreign Schools Only]

• ESC Certification with ESC ID No. & ESC School ID [For Education Service Contracting Grantee Only] • QVR Certification with Serial No. [For Senior High School Qualified Voucher Recipient Only]

2. Seal and then label at the back of the long brown envelope with the proper: • Complete Name • Student Number • Grade Level, Strand, and Section • Mobile Number • Date of Submission • List of Documents submitted
